What am I looking at?

This dashboard reports flashing traffic signals in Austin, TX. Traffic signals enter flash mode when something is preventing the signal from operating normally. This is typically the result of a power surge, power outage, or damage to signal equipment. A signal may also be placed into flash mode for maintenance purposes or be scheduled to flash overnight.
