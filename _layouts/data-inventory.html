About the Austin Transportation Open Data Inventory

Austin Transportation Department's Open Data Inventory tracks the publication status of all transportation data assets of which the Transportation Department is a steward. Datasets listed with a publication status of "Production" are available for download on the City of Austin's Open Data Portal. Contact transportation.data@austintexas.gov for additional information about the data inventory.
