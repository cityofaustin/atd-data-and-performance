What am I looking at?

This dashboard reports the progress of the Austin Transportation Department's Annual Signal Retiming Program. The goal of the Annual Signal Retiming Program is to ensure that Austin's traffic signals are timed for optimum safety and performance.

How do you choose which signals will be retimed?

We re-time roughly 1/3 third of our signals each year, such that every signal in our system will be re-timed at least once every three years.

How do you set your goals?

The Annual Signal Re-retiming Program's performance goals are established by city management, and are adjusted annually based on the prior year's performance.

How can I report an issue with a traffic signal's timing?

To report an issue or request a new traffic signal, call 3-1-1. You can also submit a traffic signal service request online.
