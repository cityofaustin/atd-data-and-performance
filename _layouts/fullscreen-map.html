About the Transportation Management Center

The Transportation Management Center (TMC) is the City of Austin's headquarters for monitoring and managing traffic throughout the Austin area. The center is staffed seven days a week through peak travel times, and may be staffed continuously through large events.

TMC engineers and operators monitor traffic conditions and adjust signal timing in response to roadway condtions. Crews are dispatched as needed to respond to downed or malfunctioning traffic signals.

To report a traffic signal issue, contact 311.
